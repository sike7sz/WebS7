§ 001 — About

What We Are

Transparency is a value we hold in the highest regard. Here is exactly what we are willing to tell you.

TsukasaYugi & Affiliates Co. LLC is a holding company, research collective, and infrastructure provider operating at the intersection of the physical, digital, and other domains. Founded under the authority of Tsukasa Yugi, the company has since expanded into a network of subsidiaries and strategic partnerships spanning multiple organizations — some of which are documented in this portal.

Our operations are guided by a philosophy of absolute persistence: systems built by this company do not fail gracefully. They do not fail at all. Every product shipped under the Tsukasa-7sTwin product line is subject to internal review before external deployment, and all hardware is identified under the vendor string TsukasaYugi & Affiliates Co. LLC.

We acknowledge that certain aspects of our operations may appear unusual to outside observers. This is intentional. The company was not designed to be comfortable. It was designed to function.
